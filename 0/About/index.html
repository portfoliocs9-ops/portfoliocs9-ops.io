About me
Precious Oluchi Kalu is a developing Cybersecurity Analyst, currently enrolled in the ALX Cybersecurity program. She is passionate about digital safety, threat analysis, and protecting people through secure technology.
Before beginning her cybersecurity journey, Precious worked in Customer Service, where she gained strong communication skills, empathy, professionalism, and the ability to solve problems calmly. These strengths now support her transition into cybersecurity, especially in incident reporting, user communication, and analytical thinking.
Precious has completed a Customer Service Support Certification (Alison) and continues to expand her technical skills through hands-on cybersecurity labs, group research, and security tools. She aims to grow into a skilled Cybersecurity Analyst who contributes to security-first organizations committed to learning and innovation.
